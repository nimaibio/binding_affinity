ML-VSPred

Machine Learning Based Models for Virtual Screening

Aboust ML-VSPred

Virtual screening is a computational technique used in the early stages of drug discovery to identify potential drug candidates from large chemical libraries. It helps narrow down the compound libraries for experimental evaluation, reducing the cost and time associated with traditional drug development. Machine learning-based virtual screening of natural compounds is transforming drug discovery by accurately predicting protein-ligand interactions. In contrast to traditional methods such as experimental assays and computational docking, machine learning models improve efficiency by utilizing large datasets and advanced algorithms. To support this, we developed the ML-VSPred web server for rapid and accurate natural product-based screening of therapeutic proteins.
